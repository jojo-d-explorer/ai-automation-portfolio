## Week 1 Key Insights

### Trade-offs in Search Depth
- CoWork defaults to first page (~10 results) per search
- Google may show "82 results" but only extracts visible ones
- Can configure depth: shallow (fast, broad) vs deep (slow, thorough)
- Decision: Use shallow for daily scans, deep for targeted searches

### Search Strategy
- 24 combinations (3 platforms × 2 titles × 4 locations) = overwhelming
- Better approach: 6 high-value combinations searched more thoroughly
- Quality over quantity in search design
- Boolean language can be confusing, be explicit about the search and potential combinations
- Specificity is key to a clean CSV. Limit the returning information wherever possible (e.g. job listings can be very long - limit to 2 sentences, include "N/A" limiter to avoid empty spaces
- Consider the order in which you want information to be presented. What makes the most sense. 
- Be explicit about where information should be saved "Save to XYZ file", then ask cowork to verify
