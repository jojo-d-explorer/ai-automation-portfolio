📋 How scoring works

Every job gets scored out of 100 based on five weighted criteria matched against your resume. Seniority carries the most weight (30 points) — the system looks for Director, VP, C-suite, and Managing Director-level roles that match your Executive Director track at JPMorgan. Skills alignment is next (25 points), scoring how well the role's requirements map to your investment banking, legal, infrastructure policy, and deal execution background. Location (20 points) rewards NYC and DC roles highest, with Remote-US also scoring well. Industry fit (15 points) prioritizes defense tech, AI/ML, energy, and cybersecurity. Salary (10 points) rounds it out — most roles don't list comp, so the system scores conservatively when it's missing. Each job has a Score Rationale column explaining exactly why it scored where it did.
