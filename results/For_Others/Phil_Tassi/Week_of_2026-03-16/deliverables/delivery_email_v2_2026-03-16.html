📍 Tab 2: Non-Queryable Companies (74) — your research opportunity

The second tab in the spreadsheet lists 74 companies where we don't yet know which ATS they use. Most came from funding intel (recently funded companies that haven't been searched on job boards yet). They're tracked in the system but the API checker can't query them until we know their ATS.

How to resolve them (takes ~30 seconds per company):

For each company, try these URLs in order:

1. https://boards-api.greenhouse.io/v1/boards/{company-slug}/jobs
2. https://api.lever.co/v0/postings/{company-slug}
3. https://api.ashbyhq.com/posting-api/job-board/{company-slug}

If any returns a JSON response (not a 404), that's their ATS.
The slug is usually the company name, lowercase, no spaces.

Example: "Vestwell" → try "vestwell" as the slug.
If greenhouse returns data, it's Greenhouse. Update companies.json.

Each company you resolve adds +1 to the API checker pool. If you resolve even 20 of the 74, that's 20 more companies being probed every week for roles matching your profile. The high-priority ones to research: Vestwell, Outpost, Entrepreneurs First, Nscale, Alan, Replit, Temporal (all recently funded, all relevant to your profile).
